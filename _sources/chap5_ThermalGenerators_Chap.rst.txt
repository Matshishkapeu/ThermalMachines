****************************
Heat pumps and refrigerators
****************************

**Heat pumps** and **refrigerators** are *thermal energy converters*: they use mechanical energy to move thermal energy from a cold source to a hot sink.

The *Carnot heat generator* (seen in section :ref:`Sec:chap2:CarnotHeatGen`) presents the maximum thermal efficiency but *Real heat generators* are generally not exchanging thermal energy using isothermal transformations.

This chapter treats:

  1) Gas heat pump and refrigeration cycle (reverse Brayton cycle)
  2) Vapor compression heat pumps and refrigerators
